Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta, savory meat or vegetable sauce, creamy ricotta or béchamel, and melted cheese—usually mozzarella or Parmesan. It's baked until golden and bubbling, creating a rich, comforting meal. Originating from Italy, lasagna has become popular worldwide and comes in many variations, including vegetarian, seafood, and even vegan styles.
